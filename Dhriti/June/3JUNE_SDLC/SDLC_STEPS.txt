:Software Development Life Cycle:

'Requirements Specification'

-Gathering business requirements from clients.
-Evaluating the feasibility of creating the product.
-Determining cost of production and needs of users.

'Architectural, Components & detailed Design'

-Creating important documents such as a software requirement 
-Creating the information gathered during planning and analysis into clear requirements documentation for the development team.
-Planning system design, programming languages & frameworks, performance &security measures.

'Implementation'

-Follow the design and protocols set by the organization.
-Start building the software using programming code

'Unit Integration & acceptence testing'

-Integrate components and test the system as a whole.
-Testing

'Installation and Maintenance'

-Build the software to the requirements.
-Bug fixing.
